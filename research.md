# GigAegis Research & Architecture

## The Problem
Gig workers lose wages during urban floods; traditional insurance doesn't cover this.

## The Solution
A parametric, automated payout system based on weather APIs.

## The Defense (Critical)
We mitigate GPS spoofing and market crash compliance using:
- **Edge-Level Telemetry:** Android isMock APIs.
- **Physical Sensor Fusion:** Deriving velocity from triaxial acceleration (v = integral of a dt).
- **Graph Neural Networks:** To flag identical Hardware IDs.
